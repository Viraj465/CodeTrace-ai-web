# SECTION 2 — ACCEPTABLE LEGAL USAGE & DATA PRIVACY POLICY

**Last Updated:** July 2, 2026

> Codetrace-ai is built on a **privacy-first architectural philosophy**. This policy explains exactly how your data flows through our system — and, more importantly, what does **not** leave your machine.

## 2.1 Our Privacy-First Promise

2.1.1. Codetrace-ai is engineered from first principles to **minimize the data footprint** of every operation. The Platform's tracking and indexing engine:
- (a) Operates **locally** on your machine during the indexing phase;
- (b) Respects your **`.gitignore`**, with a hardcoded safety net (`ALWAYS_IGNORE_DIRS`) that automatically excludes `node_modules`, `venv`, `__pycache__`, `.env` (files and directories matching `.env*` patterns), `.git`, `vendor`, `target`, `dist`, `build`, and dozens of other non-source files and directories that **never** contain the code you actually want analyzed;
- (c) Skips proprietary Codetrace-internal state under the `.codetrace/` directory;
- (d) **Stores no persistent copy** of your code on our servers by default. Indexing representations are held **in memory** for the duration of the active session and discarded upon completion.

2.1.2. We collect only what is strictly necessary to deliver the service. We do not sell, rent, lease, or commercially exploit your data in any form.

## 2.2 How Your Codebase Data Is Indexed & Processed

2.2.1. **Local Indexing Phase.** When you initiate an analysis:
- (a) The Codetrace-ai CLI walks your repository tree on your local machine;
- (b) It applies your `.gitignore` rules (merged with our hardcoded safety net) to filter out non-source files;
- (c) It parses the remaining files using language-specific grammars (tree-sitter) to extract structural metadata — function signatures, class hierarchies, import graphs, etc.;
- (d) This structural metadata, plus targeted code excerpts, is held in **transient in-memory structures** required to render Insights.

2.2.2. **LLM Transmission Phase.** When you request an AI Insight:
- (a) **Only the minimal relevant context** (e.g., the function you're asking about, its immediate dependencies) is transmitted to integrated LLM Providers over **encrypted channels (TLS 1.3)**;
- (b) The full repository is **never** sent in bulk. We do not perform whole-codebase upload.
- (c) Transmitted payloads are governed by the **enterprise-grade API terms** of our LLM Providers (see §2.5).

2.2.3. **Storage.** By default:
- (a) No raw source code is persisted on Codetrace-ai servers;
- (b) Cached Insights may be retained for up to **thirty (30) days** to support re-rendering and session continuity, after which they are purged;
- (c) You may **explicitly opt-in** to long-term storage of analysis results (e.g., for team dashboards). This opt-in is **never** the default.

2.2.4. **Logging.** Operational logs (timestamps, request counts, error codes) may be retained for up to **ninety (90) days** for security, abuse detection, and service-improvement purposes. These logs **do not contain** your source code or prompt content.
